Once in a while I feel the need to create my own cocktails. Especially when it's about a spirit used in a drink that is so common but nowhere else.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 2 spirts thyme,
    • 40 ml Cachaça,
    • 30 ml Grand Marnier,
    • 15 ml lemon juice and
    • 15 ml simple syrup.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Double-strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  4. Top glass with ▸ soda water (roughly 30 ml).
  5. Garnish with ▸ lemon wheel and/or ▸ thyme sprig.

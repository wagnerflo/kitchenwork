What kind of cocktail could you have together with your Christmas cookies? This one, of course! Also known as liquid After Eight.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 30 ml green crème de menthe,
    • 30 ml white crème de cacao,
    • 30 ml heavy cream.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with ▸ coarse-ground dark chocolate.

Credit for this goes to one Esteban Ordonez. Crazy idea, great execution, astonishing taste.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 55 ml Cachaça,
    • 20 ml lime juice,
    • 20 ml simple syrup,
    • 1-2 teaspoons ripe avocado flesh.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Double-strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with ▸ lime wheel and/or ▸ sugarcane stick.

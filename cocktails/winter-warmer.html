Feeling cold but still want your alcohol fix? This one is perfect for warming a group of visitors coming in from the outdoors.

  1. Preheat old fashined glasses with hot water.
  2. Per serving heat up and let simmer for a few minutes
    • 100 ml apple juice and
    • 1 cinnamon stick.
  3. Fill juice and stick into the old fashined glasses and to each add
    • 40 ml apricot brandy,
    • 20 ml dry gin.

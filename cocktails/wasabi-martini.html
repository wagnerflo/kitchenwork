Hot and fresh and very versatile: Fits a Sushi diner as well as a burger or no meal at all!

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 50 ml Wodka,
    • 35 ml lemon juice,
    • 10 ml simple syrup and
    • ½ teaspoon Wasabi paste.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass.

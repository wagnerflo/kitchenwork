Tikitime! And shamelessly stolen from a Cocktail Blog. The combination of flavours (sour lime and grapefruit in contrast to sweet orgeat syrup) makes this a really interesting one.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 45ml dry gin (Beefeater 24 works great),
    • 30ml Coconut Rum (Bacardi Coco; in a pinch can be substituted with Malibu),
    • 30ml lime juice,
    • 20ml white grapefruit juice,
    • 20ml orgeat,
    • small dash of Angostura Orange.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake briefly.
  3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice.

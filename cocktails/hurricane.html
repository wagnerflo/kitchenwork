Drink this one, not the one you get in New Orleans nowadays. This recipe is much closer to the original one and tastes much more like cocktail than like alcoholic fruit juice. Also it's reasonably sized.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 40 ml dark rum,
    • 20 ml passionfruit syrup,
    • 20 ml lemon juice.
  2. Fill the shaker to ⅔ with crushed ice and shake until frothy.
  3. Pour unstrained into a highball glass.
  4. Garnish with ▸ orange slice and cherry.

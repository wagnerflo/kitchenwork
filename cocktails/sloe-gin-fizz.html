Sweet but not exclusivly so. The lemon juice and the inherent bitterness of the sloe gin counter this to some extend and make the drink interesting.

Dangerous though, as you tend to not taste much of the alcohol.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 2 teaspoons simple syrup,
    • 30 ml lemon juice and
    • 70 ml high-proof sloe gin.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake briefly.
  3. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  4. Top glass with ▸ soda water (roughly 30 ml).
  5. Garnish with ▸ lemon wheel or wedge.

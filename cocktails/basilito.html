1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 8 basil leaves,
    • 2 teaspoons fine cane sugar,
    • 30 ml lime juice,
    • 45 ml white rum.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Double-strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  4. Top glass with ▸ soda water (roughly 45 ml).
  5. For Garnish mix ▸ basil leaves into the drink.

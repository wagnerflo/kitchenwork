Make a Mule but replace the ginger beer (who would even consider such a lower class beverage) with something more suitable: Champagne—or maybe sparkling wine if you're not that classy and rich.

  1. Add to a highball glass and muddle
    • 20 ml simple syrup,
    • 4 slices of fresh, peeled ginger.
  2. Squeeze and drop ▸ 2 slices of lime into the glass.
  3. Fill with ice and add ▸ 20 ml dry gin.
  4. Top with ▸ dry sparkling wine (roughly 100 ml).

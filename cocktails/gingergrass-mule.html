A cocktail born out of the desire to make use of some leftovers but quickly (like in the first two sips) turned to a favourite.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 60 ml Cachaça,
    • 30 ml lime juice.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake briefly.
  3. Strain into a double old fashioned glass half-filled with ice.
  4. Top glass with ▸ ginger beer (roughly 200 ml). Thomas Henry's is a good choice.
  5. Garnish with ▸ lime wedge or wheel.

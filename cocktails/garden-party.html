The Aperol Spritz' big brother that packs quite a bit more punch. Combines strong, sour, sweet and fresh in a single glass.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 8 basil leaves,
    • 2 teaspoons simple syrup,
    • 15 ml lemon juice,
    • 40 ml Aperol and
    • 40 ml Tanqueray Ten gin.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously..
  3. Double-strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  4. Top glass with ▸ soda water (roughly 30 ml).
  5. Garnish with ▸ lemon wheel and/or basil sprig.

Apparently this is the “… only real Hong Kong cocktail …”, whatever that is supposed to mean.

For this one to provide a well rounded taste the brands of the ingredients are rather important. Especially the spiciness of Ginger Beer and the sweetness of Ginger Ale vary immensely.

  1. In a highball glass combine
    • 100 ml Fever-Tree Ginger Beer,
    • 100 ml Thomas Henry Ginger Ale,
    • 20 ml lime juice and
    • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters.
  2. Stir briefly and top the glass with ice.
  3. Garnish with
    • lemon or lime wheel on the rim or
    • lemon or lime slices in the glass.

1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 50 ml Pisco,
    • 30 ml lemon juice,
    • 15 ml simple syrup,
    • 1 level teaspoon egg white powder.
  2. Thoroughly shake without ice to make the egg white foam more stable.
  3. Add ice to ½ of the shaker and shake vigorously.
  4. Strain into a old fashioned glass.
  5. Garnish with a ▸ dash of Angostura bitters on the foam.

My take on a favourite of mine from the Kongress Bar in Munich. This recipe was discovered to taste very much like the bar's creation when adapting the Sunflower to be none-alcoholic.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 40 ml Cream of coconut,
    • 60 ml passionfruit juice,
    • 60 ml pineapple juice,
    • 80 ml orange juice,
    • 10 ml pomegranate syrup and
    • 20 ml lime juice.
  2. Fill the shaker to Â¾ with crushed ice and shake until frothy.
  3. Pour unstrained into a Hurricane glass.
  4. Add a straw and garnish with
    • pineapple wedge and/or
    • maraschino cherry.

This one fits the same bill as the Grasshopper. Be it for dessert or when you need something for your sweet tooth.

  1. In a cocktail shaker combine
    • 30 ml cognac or brandy,
    • 30 ml white crème de cacao,
    • 15 ml heavy cream,
    • 15 ml milk.
  2. Fill the shaker to ½ with ice and shake vigorously.
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass.
  4. Add a dusting of ▸ ground nutmeg.
